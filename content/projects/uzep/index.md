**Funding Source:** TUBITAK 1001 (Project No: 120K200)

**Period:** 2020–2021

UZEP is a cloud-based distance education platform developed for use by all higher education institutions in Turkey. The hardware infrastructure was provided by ULAKBIM, while Sakarya University developed the software components. The system can be quickly deployed without requiring the IT resources of individual universities.
